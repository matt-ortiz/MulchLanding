Pricing FAQ

Common Questions

When am I billed?
Subscriptions are billed annually. You'll be charged once when you sign up, and then on the same date each year unless you cancel.
Can I change plans?
Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. The price difference will be prorated based on the time remaining in your current billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards and debit cards. For Premium plan customers, we can also accommodate purchase orders and ACH transfers.
Do you offer educational discounts?
Our pricing is already set as low as possible to ensure that fundraising proceeds go where they're intended - to your organization's goals. If pricing is an issue, please don't hesitate to reach out to us, and we'll work to find a solution that meets your needs.
Is there a free trial?
We offer a limited demo environment for schools and organizations to test our platform before subscribing. Contact us to set up a guided demo.
Are there any limits on the paid plan?
No! Our annual subscription includes unlimited deliveries, unlimited drivers, and unlimited admin accounts. We've designed the platform to scale with your needs, so your fundraiser can grow without worrying about hitting any limits.

Need a custom solution?

If you have unique requirements or are looking for a custom plan tailored to your organization's specific needs, our team is here to help.
